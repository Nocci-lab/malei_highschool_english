Part 1

① New Words 単語

  1. We heard laughter from the next room at that time.
    laughter = 笑い声
  2. I think your voice has the power to attract people.
    attract = 引きつける、魅了する
  3. I had such a curious dream.
    curious = 奇妙な、不思議な
  4. Bill is a skillful performer on the trumpet.
    performer = 演奏者、演者
  5. I had a sharp pain in my stomach last night.
    stomach = 胃、おなか
  6. Hannah's movements are elegant, probably because she is learning ballet.
    elegant = 優雅な
  7. After some reflection, I decided to accept the job offer.
    after some reflection = よく考えた末に
  8. At the school, you can learn the craft of jewelry making.
    craft = 技、工芸

② Idioms & Expressions 表現

  1. We will interview our principal tomorrow.
    interview = インタビューする
  2. After the Rugby World Cup, many people became interested in rugby.
    become interested in ~ = 〜に興味を持つ
  3. When will you come back to Japan?
    come back to ~ = 〜に戻ってくる
  4. Jason was eager to stay at a traditional Japanese inn in Kyoto.
    be eager to do = 〜したがっている

③ Grammar 時制の一致

  1. He was always saying he wanted to visit Japan's old capital.
  2. My mother said she would be coming home late.
  3. I didn't know that he had decided to study abroad.
解説
主節が過去形になると、後ろの文も過去にずれます。want → wanted、will be → would be、decided → had decided になります。

④ イラスト問題

  1. Really? I didn't know that Jill would move to Kyoto.
  2. Yes. I knew that Ken had appeared on TV last week.
解説
「will move」は、過去の会話の中では would move。「appeared」は、その時点より前のことなので had appeared が自然です。

⑤ Comprehension 内容一致

番号答え理由
(1)F大学生のときに見たのは日本ではなく、彼の国で行われた茂山家の公演です。
(2)T声やイントネーションだけでなく、動きの優雅さにも魅了されました。
(3)F彼は日本語をよく理解できなかったと言っています。

Part 2

① New Words 単語

  1. After many discussions, we decided to start a new project.
  2. I am going to translate his new novel.

  4. I was somewhat surprised to see him at the hospital.
  5. My father likes old-fashioned American cars.
  6. These windows open automatically when it is sunny.

② Idioms & Expressions 表現

  1. Please pay attention to the airport announcements.
  2. In short, he is a man of few words.
  3. She repeated what I said word for word.

③ Grammar 未来進行形 / be to do

  1. I will be climbing Mt. Fuji at this time tomorrow.
  2. We will be visiting Kinkaku-ji temple on the last day of the tour.
  3. Investigation teams are to meet the victims next week.
  4. When I first saw him, I felt I was going to get married to him someday.
解説
未来進行形は「未来のある時点で〜しているところ」を表します。be to do は「予定」を表します。was going to は「〜することになりそうだ、〜するつもりだった」という過去から見た未来です。

④ イラスト問題

  1. I'd love to, but I will be watching a movie with Carrie at that time.
  2. Could you make it a little earlier? I will be having dinner at that time.
  3. Yes. I will be relaxing by the sea at this time next week.

⑤ Comprehension 内容一致

番号答え理由
(1)F日本の狂言一座に誘われたのではなく、彼自身がチェコで一座を始めました。
(2)Tどの狂言を選ぶかについて、たくさん話し合いました。
(3)T古い日本語の響きに近づけるため、少し古風なチェコ語も使います。

② Idioms & Expressions 表現

  1. The job is great in terms of salary, but the office is too far from my house.
  2. Even though my grandmother is over 90 years old, she is still very active.
    Although でも意味は合いますが、本文表現に合わせるなら Even though。
  3. Those two cultures have something in common with each other.

③ Comprehension 内容一致

番号答え理由
(1)T狂言は人間の弱さや愚かさを描くので、どの国の人にも理解できると言っています。
(2)F太郎は黙っているのではなく、声を変えて、家にいないふりをします。
(3)F太郎は音楽が好きだからではなく、思わず歌や踊りに加わってしまい、見つかります。
